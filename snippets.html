Task management app
Project brief
Research, prototype, test, and validate an idea for a new mobile application that solves a clearly identified need in users’ everyday lives
My role
UX researcher and designer in a 3-person team
Methodology
Our initial brainstorming and research suggested that many people struggle to get their daily tasks done and feel in control of their time. We believed we could solve this with an app that:
  • Mimics the tactile satisfaction of a physical to do list
  • Limits feelings of overwhelm
  • Helps users manage time
  • Uses rewards to increase motivation
